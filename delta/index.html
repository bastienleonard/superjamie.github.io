Notes
  • Effector Offset measures the distance from effector centre (nozzle) to the arm joint axle.
  • Carriage Offset measures the distance from the extrusion face to the arm axle. Extrusion offset is added depending on which corner is in use.
  • Maximum Arm Length is the arm which achieves 60° arm angle at origin.
  • Minimum Arm Length is the arm which achieves 20° arm angle at the far edge of movement.
  • Movement Diameter just describes where the arms can reach, you'll probably hit the towers first.
